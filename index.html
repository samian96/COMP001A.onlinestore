Our Company

History

Our company was started out just trying to help out other bird owners find quality feed to keep their birds happy and healthy. It started off as just a way to inform people, and help them take care of their pets. Now we have turned into a small internet shop that is able to provide quality feed to pet bird owners around the country.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help provide healthy choices for bird owners at an affordable price. Keeping your pet birds happy and healthy all starts with feeding them high quality feed.

Services

Some of the services we provide are selling quality bird feed for different species of parrots, as well as wild birds for avid bird watchers. Other services we can help with is providing you the right information on what feed to buy. For any questions you have please feel free to contact us.
